## Competitor Profiles

**Alice** (https://alice.tech)
- Positioning: “Learn more in less time” and “Alice turns your course materials into a guided learning journey, with everything you need to succeed at university.”
- Target audience: University students studying from course materials, especially PDF-based study workflows and exam prep users.
- Content gaps: No pricing details were shown on the homepage., No evidence of workplace or professional learning use cases on the crawled page., The homepage says Alice works best with PDF files, but there was no support shown for image or picture input in chat.

**360Learning** (https://360learning.com)
- Positioning: “The learning platform that combines the power of AI with the magic of collaborative learning.”
- Target audience: L&D teams, enterprise training buyers, and organizations running compliance, onboarding, sales enablement, and frontline staff training.
- Content gaps: No consumer or individual learner positioning on the crawled homepage., No document-upload-first personal study workflow was visible on the homepage., No language-learning or academic exam-prep content was surfaced in the crawled material.

**Docebo** (https://www.docebo.com)
- Positioning: “AI-First LMS for Enterprise Learning & Skills” and “Build AI agents that get real work done.”
- Target audience: Enterprise learning teams training employees, customers, and partners, especially organizations that want AI-assisted content creation and agent workflows.
- Content gaps: No consumer study-app positioning was visible on the crawled homepage., No academic exam-prep or student-focused learning workflow was surfaced., No public self-serve pricing details were shown on the crawled page.

**Sana Labs** (https://www.sanalabs.com)
- Positioning: “Superintelligence for work” and “Give your teams the tools they need to thrive in an AI-first world.”
- Target audience: Enterprise teams buying AI-native learning, authoring, tutoring, search, and automation tools.
- Content gaps: No consumer or student-focused learning experience was visible on the crawled homepage., No public pricing details were surfaced in the fetched content., No explicit document-upload-to-personal-study workflow was shown on the homepage.

**Absorb LMS** (https://www.absorblms.com)
- Positioning: “AI-powered LMS” and “Close knowledge gaps faster with the AI-powered Absorb.”
- Target audience: Enterprise learning buyers using LMS workflows for onboarding, upskilling, compliance, customer education, partner enablement, and selling courses.
- Content gaps: No consumer study-app or classroom-style learner experience was visible on the crawled homepage., No academic test-prep positioning was surfaced., No public pricing details were shown on the fetched page.

**Duolingo** (https://www.duolingo.com)
- Positioning: “The free, fun, and effective way to learn a language!”
- Target audience: Consumer language learners, plus English test takers, school users, and kids via Duolingo ABC.
- Content gaps: No document-upload, course-authoring, or study-material conversion workflow was visible on the homepage., No workplace learning or LMS administration features were surfaced., No public pricing details were fetched on the homepage crawl.

**Coursera** (https://www.coursera.org)
- Positioning: “Learn without limits” and “Train your team in skills that scale.”
- Target audience: Career-oriented learners, students seeking degrees or certificates, and enterprise teams using Coursera for Business or Teams.
- Content gaps: No deep native course-authoring tools were visible on the homepage., No document-to-course workflow was surfaced in the crawled content., No study-group-first UX was visible on the fetched page.

**Pearson** (https://www.pearson.com)
- Positioning: “Where learning comes to life” and “Explore learning for every stage of life with Pearson.”
- Target audience: School, college, and work learners, including students using Pearson+ study tools and study prep products.
- Content gaps: No explicit AI-native tutoring promise was visible on the homepage., No public self-serve pricing details were shown on the fetched page., No course-authoring or learner-community features were surfaced.

**Arist** (https://www.arist.co)

**5Mins.ai** (https://www.5mins.ai)
